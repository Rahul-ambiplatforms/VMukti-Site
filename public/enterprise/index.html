18 Years of Computer Vision R&D in One Platform

VMukti was founded in 2007 as a video technology research company. Unlike startups that license third-party AI, every model in our VisualBot engine is trained on our own datasets, optimized for surveillance-specific scenarios, and hardened through millions of real-world deployments.

This means our AI doesn't just detect objects — it understands context. A person loitering is different from a person waiting. A vehicle stopped is different from a vehicle parked. VMukti's models reduce false alerts by up to 85% compared to generic AI platforms.
