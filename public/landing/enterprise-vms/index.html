FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does VMukti pricing work for enterprises? +
VMukti uses a simple per-camera, per-month subscription model. No upfront hardware costs, no per-feature add-ons. AI analytics, cloud storage, and multi-site management are all included. For enterprises with 500+ cameras, we offer custom volume pricing. Contact our sales team for a detailed quote.
Can I keep my existing cameras? +
Absolutely. VMukti works with any ONVIF-compliant or RTSP-capable camera. This includes Axis, Hanwha, Bosch, Hikvision, Dahua, and hundreds of other brands. There's zero rip-and-replace required — you simply point your existing cameras to VMukti.
Where is my video data stored? +
VMukti supports multi-region cloud deployment on Microsoft Azure. You choose where your data lives — US (Azure East US, West US 2), EU (West Europe, North Europe), Middle East (UAE North), UK (UK South), Asia-Pacific (Southeast Asia, Central India, Australia East), or your own private cloud via Azure Stack Hub or Azure Arc. Data residency controls are built-in for GDPR and local data sovereignty compliance.
What's the migration process from Milestone/Genetec? +
We provide a dedicated migration team and a proven 4-phase process: (1) Discovery & assessment of your current setup, (2) Parallel deployment alongside your existing VMS, (3) Gradual camera cutover with zero downtime, (4) Full transition with legacy decommission. Most enterprises complete migration in 2-4 weeks.
Is VMukti NDAA compliant? +
Yes. VMukti is fully NDAA (National Defense Authorization Act) compliant. Our software contains no components from banned entities (Hikvision, Dahua, Huawei). This makes VMukti suitable for US federal government, defense, and banking installations where NDAA compliance is mandatory.
What SLA do you offer? +
Enterprise customers receive a 99.9% uptime SLA with guaranteed response times: P1 critical issues — 30 minute response, P2 high — 2 hour response, P3 medium — 8 hour response. We provide 24/7 support via dedicated account managers, phone, email, and a priority support portal.
